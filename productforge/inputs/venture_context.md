# Venture Context: Kumbo

## Overview
**Venture Name:** Kumbo
**One-liner:** A goal-based saving and financial learning companion that helps children learn money through earning, patience, and progress—guided by parents.

## Problem Space
- **Kids:** Struggle to understand money abstractly.
- **Parents:** Need a structured, safe tool to teach saving, delayed gratification, and earning.

## Target Users (ICP)
- **Primary:** Children (6–14)
- **Secondary:** Parents/guardians who manage allowance + chores and want measurable learning progress.

## Geography
- **Primary:** Turkey first
- **Expansion:** Global

## Constraints
- **Privacy-first:** No PII exposure; anonymized analytics only.
- **Child-safe UX:** No external links, no ads to kids.
- **Simplicity:** Playful UI; low reading complexity.

## Success Metrics (KPIs)
1. First-goal activation rate (D1)
2. Goal completion rate (monthly)
3. WAU families
4. Lesson completion rate (weekly)
5. 30/60/90-day retention

## Non-Goals
- No real banking/card features in early stages.
- No social feed or child-to-child communication.
- No complex budgeting/finance jargon.

## Known Risks
- Synthetic insights mistaken for real user truth (must validate with real parents/kids).
- Over-engineering features before habit loop is proven.
- Compliance and child data safety requirements.
