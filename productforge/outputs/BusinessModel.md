# Business Model Canvas (Lean): Kumbo

## 1. Customer Segments
*   **Parents:** Digital-savvy, 30-45, urban. Value financial literacy.
*   **Children:** 6-14 years old. Gamers (Roblox/Minecraft generation).

## 2. Problem
*   Cashless society makes money abstract.
*   Kids nag for toys ("I want it now").
*   Parents lack tools to teach delayed gratification.

## 3. Revenue Streams
*   **Freemium (MVP):** Free core features (task tracking, manual wallet).
*   **Subscription (Plus):** 49.99 TL/mo.
    *   Unlimited Goals.
    *   Premium Avatars/Themes.
    *   Advanced Lessons (Investing 101).
    *   Multiple Children (3+).
*   **Future:** Affiliate (Referral to real banks/Junior cards).

## 4. Cost Structure
*   **Cloud Infrastructure:** Supabase/Vercel (Low initially).
*   **Development:** MVP build (Time/Cost).
*   **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** Marketing, Influencer partnerships.

## 5. Unique Value Prop
*   **"Simulated Wealth":** Safe environment before real banking.
*   **Gamified Education:** It's a game, not a spreadsheet.
*   **Hybrid Wallet:** Tracks Cash + Digital in one place.

## 6. Channels
*   **App Stores:** ASO (App Store Optimization).
*   **Social Media:** Instagram/TikTok (Parent influencers).
*   **Referral:** Family invite loops.

## 7. Key Metrics
*   **Active Families:** Families with at least 1 task/week.
*   **Conversion Rate:** Free -> Paid.
*   **Retention:** Month 3 retention.
