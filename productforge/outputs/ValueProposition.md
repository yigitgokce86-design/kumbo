# Value Proposition Canvas: Kumbo

## 1. Product (Kumbo)

### Products & Services
*   **Gamified Finance App:** Mobile-first web app for tracking allowance and chores.
*   **Hybrid Wallet:** Tracks both digital balance (owed by parent) and physical cash (entered manually).
*   **Family Dashboard:** Central command center for parents to manage multiple kids.

### Gain Creators (How we create value)
*   **Visualization:** Turns abstract numbers into concrete progress bars (Target: LEGO Set).
*   **Conflict Reduction:** Provides a neutral 3rd party system for "Can I have this?" conversations.
*   **Education:** Teaches "Earning = Effort" via the Task/Reward loop.
*   **Status/Fun:** Badges and Streaks make saving feel like leveling up in a game.

### Pain Relievers (How we solve problems)
*   **No PII/KYC Required:** Removes the "Identity Theft" fear barrier for onboarding.
*   **Automatic Tracking:** No more "Did I give you your allowance this week?" disputes.
*   **Safe Playground:** Simulates banking without the risk of real overdrafts or lost cards.

## 2. Customer (Parent & Child)

### Gains (What they want)
*   **Child:** Wants autonomy, toys/games, feeling "grown up".
*   **Parent:** Wants financially responsible kids, peace of mind, less nagging.

### Pains (What hurts)
*   **Child:** Waiting is boring. Money is invisible. Parents say "no" arbitrarily.
*   **Parent:** Forgetting allowance. Not knowing how to teach finance. Handling cash is annoying.

### Customer Jobs
*   **Parent:** "Teach my kid money skills without it being a chore."
*   **Child:** "Get the stuff I want."
